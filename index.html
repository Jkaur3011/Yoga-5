MY INSPIRATIONS


Ramkrishna Yadav

Ramkrishan Yadav, also known as Baba Ramdev is an Indian yoga teacher. Swami Ramdev ji was born to Smt. Gulab Devi and Shri Ram Niwas in a village of Haryana. He had his early education in a village school. At the age of 14 he was admitted to the Gurukul at Kalwa (near Jind, Haryana) where under the blessed tutelage of Ācārya Shri Baldevji he studied Sanskrit and Yoga. Quite early in his life he had his goals cut out for him, so he chose the path of celibacy and asceticism. After doing a stint of teaching Yoga, Pāṇini’s Aṣṭādhyāyī and Patañjali’s Mahabhāṣya at Gurukuls, he set out on his journey to the Gangotri caves of lofty Himalayas, away from the distractions of mundane activities.



Shilpa Shetty

Shilpa Shetty,(born Ashwini Shetty; 8 June 1975) is an Indian actress, film producer, dancer, author, businesswoman and a former model. She is also a Yogini. In an exclusive interview with News24, Shilpa spoke in detail about the benefits of yoga and meditation. Talking in detail about yoga, Shilpa said that she started practicing yoga almost 18 years back when she was suffering from neck pain issues. The 'Dhadkan' actress revealed that she hired a yoga teacher to help her in curing the disease. But later, yoga became part of her life.
